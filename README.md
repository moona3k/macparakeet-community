# MacParakeet

**The fastest, most private voice app for Mac.**

Speak anywhere on macOS and your words become text — instantly, locally, privately. Transcribe files and YouTube videos with a single drag-and-drop. No cloud. No subscription. No account.

**[$49 one-time purchase](https://macparakeet.com) · 7-day free trial**

## Two Modes, One App

### System-Wide Dictation

Press your hotkey in any app — Mail, Slack, VS Code, anything — and start talking.

- **Double-tap** for persistent recording (tap again to stop)
- **Hold** for push-to-talk (release to stop)
- Text is pasted automatically; your clipboard is never overwritten
- Compact overlay shows waveform and timer without stealing focus

### File & YouTube Transcription

Drag audio or video files onto the app (or the menu bar icon) and get a full transcript.

- MP3, WAV, M4A, FLAC, MP4, MOV, MKV, and more
- Paste a YouTube URL for instant local transcription
- Export as TXT, Markdown, SRT, VTT, DOCX, PDF, or JSON
- Word-level timestamps and confidence scores
